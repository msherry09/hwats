# Health and Wellness Assessment Tracking System 
**Deveoplment**: April - October 2012

**Project Summary**: Tracking system for tracking the Health And Wellness Assessment(HWA). The HWA is a combined effort of the Williams County Health Department(WCHD), Midwest Community Health Associates (MCHA) and Community Hospitals and Wellness Centers(CHWC) designed to help people understand their risk factors for diabetes and weight management. Pages are created dynamically based on the number of selected questions in a MySQL database. Design reporting pages to show results on individual participants and overall.

**Languages Used**: PHP, HTML, Javascript, jQuery, AJAX, CSS, MySQL
